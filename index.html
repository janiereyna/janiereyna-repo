About
Janie

I'm a software engineering student with a passion for creating innovative solutions through technology. Pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from University of North Texas, I have gained a solid foundation in programming principles and software development methodologies.

As a student, I have honed my skills through various courses, projects, and internships. I have worked on a website called "Color-Vision" and an "UNT Rideshare App", which have allowed me to gain practical experience in web and mobile app development. Additionally, I am currently working towards earning my Cybersecurity certificate and a minor in mathematics. I am excited about the endless possibilities technology offers and the ability to make a positive impact on society.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy spending time with my furry companion as a proud dog mom. In my free time, I like to stay active and I am also a food enthusiast and love making mexican dishes I learned from my grandmother.
